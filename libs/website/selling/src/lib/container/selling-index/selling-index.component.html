Selling

Selling at auction

Getting Started

If you're new, register yourself on the website first. After this, you can select 'Sell an item' to register as a Seller.

List your Item

Create a listing for the item you would like to sell. Upload good quality photos and add a comprehensive description. Reply to all questions potential buyers may ask.

When you receive an order, wait for the payment to reflect in your or bank account.

Fulfill Order

The transactions made on Bush Trading are legally binding. Ship the item and e-mail the tracking code or waybill number to the buyer. Don't forget to rate the buyer.

Photo tips

Here are some tips anyone with ANY camera can begin using today to improve the quality of their shots for online sales.

  1. Select the right camera
  2. Shoot from a tripod or stable surface
  3. Decide on natural or artificial light - choose one
  4. Set your backdrop
  5. Remove distracting elements
  6. Use simple staging (that won't distract from your product)
  7. Use a reflective surface
  8. Shoot a variety of images
