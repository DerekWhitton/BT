About Bush Trade

Bush Trade came about after a need was identified without an easy solution being available. The idea was dreamed up by a hunter and guide during a conversation after a day of guided hunting while enjoying a relaxing GnT in the bush.

Bush trading is an online community to connect like minded people who have a passion for nature, conservation and hunting. It aims to engage people in finding expert advice, sharing reviews, finding the right equipment, destinations and the necessary connections.

It will be a community providing access to all the touch points in bringing hunters and traders together.
